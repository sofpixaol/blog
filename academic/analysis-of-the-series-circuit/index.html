滑动变阻器与定值电阻串联的电路图像分析

前言

最近我们物理老师讲到这个知识点，它有些难但又常考，并且涉及很多数学。赶着抄下一堆笔记后，感觉还是未完全理解，写下这篇blog，一是方便自己复习理解，同时也是为大家提供一份清晰的电子资料。此文会详细附上电路图、每个变量的含义、函数推导过程、函数定义域、函数图像。

电路图与变量

常量 释义
$U$ 电源电压
$R_0$ 电阻$R_0$的阻值
$R_{p\ max}$ 滑动变阻器$R_p$的最大阻值
变量 释义
$I$ 电流
$U_0$ 定值电阻$R_0$两端电压
$U_p$ 滑动变阻器$R_p$两端电压
$R_p$ 滑动变阻器$R_p$接入电路的阻值
$P_0$ 定值电阻$R_0$的功率
$P_p$ 滑动变阻器$R_p$的功率
$P_{total}$ 电路的总功率

将这些变量分别作为函数和自变量，根据公式$C_7^2=\frac{7!}{2!(7-2)!}=21$，一共有21种搭配，对应21种函数和图像，其中有一次函数6个，二次函数7个，反比例函数4个，其他函数4个。

接下来将分别展示这些函数的推导过程，其结果，其定义域，以及其图像。函数中的自变量用mathtt字体标明。

一次函数

  • $I - P_{total}$

$$\begin{align}
P_{total} = U\mathtt{I} \quad (0,\frac{U}{R_0}]
\end{align}$$

  • $U_p - P_{total}$

$$\begin{align}
P_{total} &= UI \\
& = U\frac{U-\mathtt{U_p}}{R_0} \\
& = -\frac{U}{R_0}\mathtt{U_p}+\frac{U^2}{R_0}
\end{align}$$

$$\therefore P_{total} = -\frac{U}{R_0}\mathtt{U_p}+\frac{U^2}{R_0} \quad [0,U)$$

  • $U_0 - P_{total}$

$$\begin{align}
P_{total} &= UI \\
& =U\cdot\frac{\mathtt{U_0}}{R_0} \\
& = \frac{U}{R_0}\mathtt{U_0} \\
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_{total}=\frac{U}{R_0}\mathtt{U_0} \quad (0,U]$$

  • $I - U_p$

$$\begin{align}
U_p &= U - U_0 \\
& = U - \mathtt{I}R_0 \\
& = -R_0\mathtt{I}+U \\
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore U_p = -R_0\mathtt{I}+U \quad (0,\frac{U}{R_0}]$$

  • $U_0 - U_p$

$$\begin{align}
U_p &= U - \mathtt{U_0} \\
& = - \mathtt{U_0} + U
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore U_p = - \mathtt{U_0} + U \quad (0,U]$$

  • $U_0 - I$

$$I = \frac{\mathtt{U_0}}{R_0} \quad (0,U]$$

二次函数

(注：这些函数的推导过程的最后一步往往是配方，即将一般式化为顶点式，便于求函数的最值)

  • $I - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
P_p &= P_{total} - P_0 \\
& = U\mathtt{I} - \mathtt{I^2}R_0 \\
& = -R_0(\mathtt{I}─\frac{U}{2R_0})^2 + \frac{U^2}{4R_0}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = -R_0(\mathtt{I}─\frac{U}{2R_0})^2 + \frac{U^2}{4R_0} \quad (0,\frac{U}{R_0}]$$

  • $U_p - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
P_p &= \mathtt{U_p}I \\
& = \mathtt{U_p}\frac{U-\mathtt{U_p}}{R_0} \\
& = -\frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_p}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = -\frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_p}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0} \quad [0,U)$$

  • $U_0 - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
\because & P_p = -\frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_p}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0}, \
& \mathtt{U_p} = U - \mathtt{U_0} \\
\therefore P_p &= -\frac{1}{R_0}(U - \mathtt{U_0}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0} \\
&= -\frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_0}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = -\frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_0}-\frac{U}{2})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0} \quad (0,U]$$

  • $P_{total} - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
P_p &= \mathtt{P_total} - P_0 \\
& = \mathtt{P_total} - I^2R_0 \\
& = \mathtt{P_total} - (\frac{\mathtt{P_{total}}}{U})^2R_0 \\
& = \mathtt{P_total} - \frac{R_0}{U^2}{\mathtt{P_{total}}}^2 \\
& = -\frac{R_0}{U^2}(\mathtt{P_{total}}-\frac{U^2}{2R_0})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = -\frac{R_0}{U^2}(\mathtt{P_{total}}-\frac{U^2}{2R_0})^2+\frac{U^2}{4R_0}$$

  • $I - P_0$

$$P_0 = R_0\mathtt{I^2}$$
$$\therefore P_0 = \mathtt{I^2}R_0 \quad (0,\frac{U}{R_0}]$$

  • $U_0 - P_0$

(公式有未知问题无法渲染)

  • $U_p - P_0$

$$\begin{align}
P_0 &= I^2R_0 \\
& = (\frac{U-\mathtt{U_p}}{R_0})^2R_0 \\
& = \frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_p}-U)^2
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_0 = \frac{1}{R_0}(\mathtt{U_p}-U)^2 \quad [0,U)$$

反比例函数

(注：虚线部分是能看到完整的曲线方便理解，没有实际意义)

  • $R_p - P_{total}$

$$P_{total} = \frac{U^2}{\mathtt{R_p}+R_0} \quad [0,R_{p\ max}]$$

  • $R_p - P_0$

$$\begin{align}
P_0 &= I^2R_0 \\
& = (\frac{U}{\mathtt{R_p}+R_0})^2R_0 \\
& = \frac{U^2R_0}{(\mathtt{R_p}+R_0)^2}
\end{align}$$
$$P_0 = \frac{U^2R_0}{(\mathtt{R_p}+R_0)^2} \quad [0,R_{p \ max}]$$

  • $R_p - U_0$

$$U_0 = \frac{UR_0}{\mathtt{R_p}+R_0} \quad [0,R_{p\ max}]$$

其他函数

  • $R_p - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
P_p &= I^2\mathtt{R_p} \\
& = (\frac{U}{R_0+\mathtt{R_p}})^2\mathtt{R_p} \\
& = \frac{U^2\mathtt{R_p}}{(\mathtt{R_p}+R_0)^2} \\
& = \frac{U^2}{\frac{(\mathtt{R_p}+R_0)^2}{\mathtt{R_p}}} \\
& = \frac{U^2}{\frac{(\mathtt{R_p}-R_0)^2}{\mathtt{R_p}}+4R_0}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = \frac{U^2}{\frac{(\mathtt{R_p}-R_0)^2}{\mathtt{R_p}}+4R_0} \quad [0,R_{p\ max}]$$

$$
\text{结论一：当}R_p=R_0\text{时，}P_p\text{有最大值}\frac{U^2}{4R_0}
$$
$$
\text{结论二：当存在}R_1\text{使得}P_p = P_x\text{时，若存在}R_2\text{也使得}P_p = P_x\text{，}
$$
$$
\text{则一定有}R_1 \times R_2 = R_0^2
$$

  • $P_0 - P_p$

$$\begin{align}
P_p &= P_{total} - \mathtt{P_0} \\
& = UI - \mathtt{P_0} \\
& = U\sqrt{\frac{\mathtt{P_0}}{R_0}} - \mathtt{P_0} \\
& = \frac{U\sqrt{\mathtt{P_0}}}{\sqrt{R_0}} - \mathtt{P_0} \\
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore P_p = \frac{U\sqrt{\mathtt{P_0}}}{\sqrt{R_0}} - \mathtt{P_0} \quad (0,\frac{U^2}{R_0}]$$

  • $P_0 - P_{total}$

$$\begin{align}
P_{total} &= UI \\
& = U\sqrt{\frac{\mathtt{P_0}}{R_0}} \\
& = \frac{U\sqrt{\mathtt{P_0}}}{\sqrt{R_0}}
\end{align}$$
$$P_{total} = \frac{U\sqrt{\mathtt{P_0}}}{\sqrt{R_0}} \quad (0,\frac{U^2}{R_0}]$$

  • $R_p - U_p$

$$\begin{align}
U_p &= I\mathtt{R_p} \\
& = \frac{U\mathtt{R_p}}{R_0+\mathtt{R_p}} \\
& = \frac{U}{\frac{R_0}{\mathtt{R_p}}+1}
\end{align}$$
$$\therefore U_p = \frac{U}{\frac{R_0}{\mathtt{R_p}}+1} \quad [0,R_{p\ max}]$$
