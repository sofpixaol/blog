常用数学运算和符号的英文读法

计数方法

  • 小数(decimal; decimal number)
    $0.5$: zero point five; point five.
    $10.875$: ten point eight seven five.
  • 小数舍入(rounding)
    $1.2735$: rounded to 3 decimal places is $1.274$.
  • 分数(fraction; fraction number)
    $a\over{b}$: a over b.
    $\frac{1}{2}$: one half.
    $\frac{1}{100}$: one hundredth.
    分子: numerator; 分母: denominator.
  • 百分数(percentage)
    $10%$: ten percent.
  • 科学计数(scientific notation)
    $1.23×10^3$: 1.23 times ten to the power of three; 1.23 times ten to the third (参考幂运算).

基本运算

  • 四则运算(Elementary arithmetic)
    $a+b=c$: a plus b equals c (plus是介词).
    $a−b=c$: a minus b equals c (minus是介词); b substracted from a leaves c.
    $a×b=c$: a times b equals c (times是介词); a multiplied by b is c.
    $a÷b=c$: a divided by b equals c.
    $1/x$: the reciprocal of x.
    $3:5$: three to five.
  • 乘方/幂运算(exponentiation)
  • $a^n$: a to the n-th (power); a to the power of n; a raised to the n-th power.
    $x^2$: x squared.
    $y^3$: y cubed.
    指数: exponent.
    底数: base.
  • 开方(radication)
    $\sqrt{16}$: the square root of 9.
    $\sqrt[3]{8}$: the cube root of 8.
    $\sqrt[n]{b}$: the n-th root of b.
  • 对数(logarithm)
    $\log_b x$: the logarithm of x to base b.
    $\log x, \ln x, \log_e x$: the natural logarithm of x.
    $\lg x, \log_10 x$: the common logarithm of x.
    $\text{lb}, x, \log_2 x$: the binary logarithm of x.
  • 阶乘(factorial)
    $5!$: five factorial.

基本符号

$()$ (parentheses)
$(2x−6y)−10$: the quantity two x minus six y, close quantity, minus ten;paren, two x minus six y, close paren, minus ten; left paren, two x minus six y, right paren, minus ten.
$a(x−y)$: a, parenthesis, x minus y, close parenthesis.
$[]$: brackets
${}$: brace
$/$: slash
$\backslash$ backslash
$#$: hash; number sign, pound sign
$&$: ampersand
$a & b$: a and b.
$*$: asterisk, star
$x^*$: x star; x asterisk.
$†$: dagger
$’$: apostrophe
$a′$: a prime (US); a dash (UK).
$a′′$: a double prime (US); a double dash (UK).
$\cdots$ ellipsis
$12+14+18+\cdots$: one half plus one quarter plus one eighth and so forth.
$!$: exclamation mark
$,$: comma
$.$: full stop, period
$?$: question mark

特殊数学符号

$\pm$: plus-minus
$\propto$: is proportional to
$\infty$: infinity

  • 上下标: superscript, subscript
    $a_1$: a (sub) one.
    $ε_ijk$: epsilon (sub) ijk.
    $a^i$: a super i.
  • 附加符号(diacritic)
    $\bar{a}$: a bar.
    $\tilde{x}$: x tilde.
    $\hat{a}$: a hat.
    $\dot{y}$: y dot.
    $\vec{x}$: vector x.
  • 逻辑运算符号
    $=$: equal, is equal to
    $\ne$ (is not equal to
    $>$: is greater than, is larger than
    $<$: is less than, is smaller than
    $\ge$: is greater than or equal to
    $\le$: is less than or equal to
    $\approx$: is approximately equal to
    $:=$ : is set to be, is defined to be
  • 代数与函数运算符号
    • 绝对值(absolute value)
    • 求和(summation)
      $\sum_{k=1}^n k^2$: the summation over k from 1 to n of k squared; the summation of k squared over k from 1 to n.
    • 求积(product)
      $\prod_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{k}$: the product over k from 1 to n of one over k.
    • 函数(function)
      $f(x)$: f of x.
      参数、变量: argument.
      反函数(inverse function)

微积分符号

  • 极限(limit)
    $\lim_{x \to 0}f(x)$: the limit as x approaches zero of f of x; the limit of f of x as x approaches zero.
  • 导数(derivative)
    $f′(x)$: f prime of x; the derivative of f with respect to x.
    ${\rm{d}y\over\rm{d}x}$: d y over d x; the derivative of y with respect to x.
    $\partial x \over \partial y$: partial y over partial x; d y over d x; the partial derivative of y with respect to x.
  • 积分(integral)
    $\int_{0}^{\infty} f(t)\rm{d}t$: the integral from t equals 0 to t equals infinity of f of t d t; the integral of f of t d t as t goes from 0 to infinity; integrate f of t d t from 0 to infinity.
